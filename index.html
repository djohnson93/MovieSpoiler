Superbad

Released: 2007

Starring:

  • Jonah Hill and Michael Cera

Movie Quote

Evan: [examining the fake ID] Hawaii. All right, that's good. That's hard to trace, I guess. Wait... you changed your name to... McLovin?

Plot

Two high school seniors trying to go out with a 'bang' before school ends. These two best friends are losers and sit alone at the lunch table. But, before school ends, they want to at least get into a party, get the girl, and just be remembered.
